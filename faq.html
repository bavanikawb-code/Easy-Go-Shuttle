Frequently Asked Questions

How to book airport transfer? expand_more
Booking is simple through our digital platform. Enter your pickup location, destination, and flight details. Select your preferred vehicle class, and complete your reservation with our secure payment gateway. You'll receive an instant confirmation via email and SMS.
Where can I find the cheapest airport transfers in Paris? expand_more
While we specialize in premium experiences, we offer competitive rates for early bookings. Our shared luxury shuttles provide the balance of cost-efficiency and comfort. For the best rates in Paris, we recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance.
How much does a taxi from Charles de Gaulle to Paris cost? expand_more
A standard taxi from CDG to central Paris typically ranges between €55 and €70. Our private transfers offer a fixed-rate guarantee starting at €65, including professional meet-and-greet services and flight monitoring, ensuring no surprises even if your flight is delayed.
What is the difference: private transfer vs shuttle transfer? expand_more

Private Transfer: Exclusive use of the vehicle for you and your group. Direct route to your destination with zero intermediate stops.

Shuttle Transfer: A cost-effective shared service where you share the vehicle with other travelers. This may include multiple stops before reaching your specific location.
